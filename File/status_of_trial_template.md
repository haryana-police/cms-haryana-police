# Status of Trial - Standard Template

Whenever generating or providing details for the "Status of Trial", use the following standardized formats.

---

### Format 1: Charges Framed
**Use this if the charges have already been framed by the court.**

FIR NO [FIR_NO] PS [POLICE_STATION] ([PETITIONER_NAME/ACCUSED])

The current status of the trial is that the Ld. Trial Court, [LOCATION], has framed charges against the accused, including [ACCUSED_NAME], under sections [SECTIONS] of the IPC on [DATE]. The case is now at the stage of [STAGE (e.g., Prosecution Evidence)], with a total of [TOTAL_WITNESSES] witnesses listed for the case; [COUNT] key prosecution witnesses, [WITNESS_NAMES], have been examined as of the report date, and the next date of hearing in the case is fixed for [NEXT_DATE].

---

### Format 2: Charges Not Yet Framed / Pending Arguments
**Use this if the case is still at the stage of framing charges/arguments.**

FIR NO [FIR_NO] PS [POLICE_STATION] ([PETITIONER_NAME])

The current status of the trial for the petitioners ([PETITIONER_NAMES]) is that charges have not been framed by the Ld. JMIC/Court, [LOCATION], and the case is fixed for arguments on charge, with the next date of hearing set for [NEXT_DATE]. [If co-accused exists: "Separately, the case against the co-accused [NAME], a Child in Conflict with Law, is pending before the Ld. Justice Juvenile Board, [LOCATION], at the stage of prosecution evidence, with the next date fixed for [DATE]."]

---

*Note: Replace all bracketed items [LIKE_THIS] with actual case data.*
